DeWalt DCK266P2-GB 18V XR Brushless Combi Drill & Impact Driver Twin Kit (2x 5.0Ah Batteries)

The DeWalt DCK266P2-GB 18V XR Brushless Twin Kit is a comprehensive set that includes a DCD796 Combi Drill and a DCF887 Impact Driver, both equipped with brushless motors for superior performance and durability. The combi drill offers versatile drilling and hammer drilling capabilities, while the impact driver provides high torque for demanding fastening applications. The kit comes with two 5.0Ah batteries, providing ample power and extended runtime — an excellent choice for professionals and DIY enthusiasts looking for a versatile and reliable cordless drilling and fastening solution.
